About

I'm an independent researcher developing Constraint‑Oriented Emergent Computation (COEC)—a cross‑domain framework connecting AI security, cryptography, scientific computing, and systems biology. My work combines formal constraints, topology, and intention metrics (semantic density, redundancy, alignment) to build scalable, verifiable systems.
